## Description Section

**Body text:**
Music Explorer is a study tool for browsing a hand-picked collection of tracks
across rock, jazz, and electronic genres. Each entry includes the artist, album,
genre, and release year — giving you a quick way to discover new music or revisit
old favourites. Use the Gallery to search and filter the full collection.
